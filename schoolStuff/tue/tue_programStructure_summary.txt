Core program
in field you want to specialize in. 
75% of degree program
 - learn what impact technology can have on society. 
  - how can you make sure that people will actually use a care robot or self-driving car? 
  - ethical questions
  -> two Impact of Technology (ITec) courses throughout program

Free electives
 - 25% of program
 - regardless of department
 - personalize your development as an engineer
 - specialize in a single field/particular theme 
  - A.I., health, energy, sustainability
 - or combine several fields of study
 - You can also choose additional ITec courses in your elective space. 
 - decision-making supported by teachers
 
Challenge-Based learning
= work on real-world challenges
	 - How do we ensure clean, affordable energy? 
	 - Sufficient housing and food for all? 
	 - How can we age healthy as life expectancy increases? 
 - search for solutions within these themes
 - work with fellow students and a professor, cross-program
 - differt types of education
  - inspiring lectures, 
  - small-scale instructional meetings
  - guided self-study. 
 - you will actively seek additional knowledge

Honors Academy
Are you looking for some additional challenge during your bachelor’s degree program? Then our Honors Academy may be just the thing for you. The Honors Academy gives you the opportunity to join a multidisciplinary student team and work on a project that can really make a difference. This means you not only get to work with students from your own program, but also with students from other programs. The first car that runs on formic acid, an motorcycle, house drone or a biosensor that detects disease – all innovations from Honors Academy students. The Honors tracks are in addition to your regular course load and will earn you 30 additional ECTS credits.
